Pokemon Type Effectiveness Calculator

How to Use

This calculator will tell you how effectve your Pokemon's move will be agaisnt the opponent you are attacking, based on the attack itself and the defending Pokemon's type. To use the calculator, first choose the attack type you plan to use. Then pick the type (or types) of the opposing Pokemon. The results will update below, telling you how effective the move is and how much the damage will be increased or decreased!
